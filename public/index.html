Welcome To My Digital Space

Hi! My name is Mason Conelius and I am an aspiring Software Engineer/Data Analyst. I'm currently studying a Bachelor of Software Engineering
at Torrens University Australia. I made this static website with the goal of having an online portfolio demonstrating my knowledge. However
while planning and learning the ropes of web development, I realised I could also incorporate many of my passions, turning this site into both
a portfolio and a passion project which now acts as an outlet for me to express my opinions toward my favourite things.
My various passions that I decided to include into this project are: gaming, music, tv shows, esports, soccer/football, and travelling.
I really enjoy the organisational aspect of creating this website, including the correct hierarchical order of headings and subheadings,
determining appropriate colour patterns and design layouts, and expressing my opinions on the categories covered through writing them down
and creating tables. For me, I view this site as both a professional portfolio, and as a healthy outlet to vent my frustrations and/or enjoyment
on a variety of topics that I follow closely. Even if nobody end up viewing this peronsal wiki, I still find it comforting believing there are
people out there that are lsitening to what I have to say about what I find interesting and entertaining.
